Daniel Keyes: 1927 - 2014

Daniel Keyes was an American Science Fiction writer, active in 1950's through to 2014, ending with his death.
His most popular works were: Flowers for Algernon and The Minds of Billy Milligan.

A few interesting facts:

  • In his late teens and early 20's, he worked as a Purser on oil tankers.
  • He pursed medicine and finally received a Bachelor's in Psychology.
  • He enjoyed writing for pulp magazines and comic books.
  • He taught creative writing at Wayne State University.
